Operating Principles

How can you work together as a team more effectively from the start?
Operating Principles provide a structure for the team to draft 4-6 ground rules everyone can agree to abide by.

Preparation

This play will take 20 minutes to complete.

What I need:

  • Annotation tool on Zoom.
  • Operating Principles play.

How to play:

  1. Schedule a 20 minute team session.
  2. Review the Operating Principles play before the session. The download is editable so that you can capture the agreed ground rules.
  3. Follow-up: revisit your commitment to the ground rules on a regular basis or after any changes to the team.
