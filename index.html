Core Competencies

Preparation

This play will take 50 minutes to complete.

Guidance

  1. Schedule a 50 minute Zoom session.
  2. Select a team leader to share the screen and to click on the thumb buttons (depending on how the team votes for each competency).
  3. The leader reads the behavior description and team members vote with a thumbs up, thumbs down or thumbs sideways within Zoom (all cameras need to be on).
  4. Leader clicks on "not sure" for a behavior description of "needs focus" if there isn't much alignment on a competency.
  5. Once the team has aligned, the leader clicks the appropriate button.
  6. At the end of the session, vote on two areas of focus for the next quarter.

What is the SCARF modal and how can it help us better regulate our emotions?

Prepare

Better Conversations

Research has repeatedly shown that labeling emotions helps to dampen the limbic system.

SCARF is easy to remember and can help us notice a threat occuring in real time. It gives us the language to label and better regulate our emotions.

  • Status is about the relative importance to others
  • Certainty is about being able to predict outcomes
  • Autonomy provides a sense of control over events
  • Relatedness is a sense of safety with others, friend v foe
  • Fairness is a perception of fiar exchange between people

Preparation

What I need:

  2. Watch Learning about the brain changes everything.
  3. Read Managing with the brain in mind.
